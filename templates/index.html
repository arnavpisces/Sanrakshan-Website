OUR GREEN OBJECTIVE

To protect and preserve the environment by plantation devices including beautification by planting trees on the Roadside and by maintaining the already green areas.

To plant medicinal plants and trees in the Green Belt of the NCT of Delhi.

To conduct rallies to create awareness amongst the citizens of India.

To organize seminars, in various functions, class, colloquium, discussion on the environment educational institutions and to associate students / children with the environment savior programmes.

To organize seminars, functions, class, colloquium, discussion for providing legal knowledge to the students in various educational institutions said to associate students / children with the knowledge of Law in environment savior programmes.

To create awareness for presenting natural resources such as water, soil, air, solar energy etc. through seminars, functions, class, colloquium, discussion etc.

To promote the conservation of water and to construct, maintain rain water harvesting plants in various localities / Institutions / Govt. Offices / societies / residential areas.

To promote the use of Solar Energy and to construct, maintain Solar Energy Plants in various localities / Institutions / Govt. Offices / societies / residential areas.

Providing for grants, scholarships, fellowships and other forms of financial assistance to the needy and deserving students for pursuing education, vocational training, skill development etc.

Granting of financial assistance to any educational institution for granting scholarships, prizes, medals, awards for excellence in preserving environment and scientific research, distribution of books and note books for poor and deserving students.

Providing food and water to the animals / birds in the Ridge areas of the NCT Delhi and plantation of Fruit plants and for maintaining them.

Providing for or contributing to education and scientific research and development in the field of environment.

To promote recycling of the waste materials in various localities / Institutions / Govt. Offices / societies / residential areas..
